Welcome to my website. I'm a third year engineering student at the University of Waterloo in Canada. I am studying Mechatonics (electro-mechanical) engineerng. I love programming and robotics. I have a blog where I have been writing product reviews and creating tutorials. My vision for my blog is to have it become an educational resource for students of all ages to learn and get ideas from my past projects.

I'm interested in robotics, telecommunications, and IoT. My current on-going projects include hexapods, quadcopters, and robotic arms.

If you're interested in checking out my projects please visit my blog! I have provided detailed tutorials for how you can do a similar project yourself.
